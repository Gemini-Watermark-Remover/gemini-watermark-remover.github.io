How It Works

Google's Gemini AI uses a technology called SynthID to embed digital watermarks into the pixels of generated images. Unlike traditional visible logos, these are often invisible or extremely subtle.

Our tool uses Reverse Alpha Blending. By mathematically isolating the watermark's transparency layer (the alpha map), we can accurately calculate the original pixel values hidden beneath, restoring your image losslessly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this tools free to use?

Yes, GeminiClear is completely free and open source. We believe everyone should have access to clean versions of their AI creations.

Q: Does it work on all images?

It is specifically optimized for images generated by Google Gemini. It detects the unique SynthID patterns and removes them precisely.

Q: Will it lower image quality?

No. Unlike AI 'inpainting' which guesses pixels, our tool uses math to reverse the watermark blending, meaning you get the exact original pixels back.

Q: Can I use it on mobile?

Absolutely! The site is fully responsive and works perfectly on iPhones, Androids, and tablets directly in your mobile browser.
